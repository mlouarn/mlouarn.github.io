Science en Cour[t]s

Science en Cour[t]s is a french short popularization movies competition based in Rennes. The goal is to present either ones PhD thesis, a scientific question or subject in a short movie of five minutes or less.
We presented a short in 2018 to take a look at the evolution of search methods. The short was entirely shot, acted and edited by the team composed of thee PhD students: Amaury Louarn, Méline Wery and myself. The movie was awarded with the third jury prize.
It can be seen just below or on the Youtube page of Science en Cour[t]s

LCodent LCréent

L Codent L Créent is an initiative from Lille (France) to raise awareness among junior high school (secondary) student, focusing on girls, to programming. Those workshop are mentored by women doing PhD in computer sciences with various background. This is based on creativity, as the code is meant to create a graphical picture, using basis of programming.
You can see the works of the Rennes’ students here: LCLC. I participated as a mentor in 2019 and again in 2020 at the Bourgchevreuil junior high school (Cesson-Sévigné (France))
